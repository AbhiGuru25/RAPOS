What is Investor Risk Profiling?

Risk profiling is the foundation of every successful investment strategy. It involves a comprehensive assessment of your financial situation, investment experience, time horizon, financial goals, and psychological tolerance for losses. Our scientifically-designed questionnaire determines your unique risk profile and ensures all investment recommendations align with your true comfort level.

Five Standard Risk Profiles

1

Conservative

Prioritize capital preservation with minimal risk expose. Expected return: 2-4%. Low volatility.

2

Moderate

Seek steady growth with controlled risk levels. Expected return: 4-6%. Moderate volatility.

3

Balanced

Balance growth aspirations with reasonable risk exposure. Expected return: 6-8%. Medium volatility.

4

Aggressive

Pursue strong growth with higher volatility acceptance. Expected return: 8-10%. High volatility.
